Contribute Subtitles

Are you a JAM translator who would like to contribute subtitles?
We would love to add to our list of available subtitles, to allow JO1's charms to reach more international JAMs!

To contribute subtitles:

DM ArchiveForJAM with the following:

• Your subtitle file (.srt format)
• The url to the original YouTube video (only videos from official accounts, and note that YouTube Shorts are not supported)
• The name you would like us to specify in the credits (+ the link to your page/site if it's different from the account you are DM-ing from)

Note that an admin may QC your subtitles to ensure accuracy, we hope you understand! :)

What videos need subbing?

Most of their older videos from 2020 currently do not have official subs, and since they're pretty old we doubt official subs will be added.
Other videos include but are not limited to:

• OffCamJO1 series
• PROCESS JO1 (EP. 23 ~ 27)
• ECC presents JO1
• Livestream videos

Do double-check with the videos we currently have on this site, as we can only display one subtitle set.

However, feel free to submit subs of videos that do have official subs (since the quality is questionable at times), as it can be provided as an extra option to JAMs.
