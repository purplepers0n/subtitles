This site allows JO1's official YouTube videos to be viewed with English subtitles, while having the views counted towards the original video! The subtitles here are created by JAM, for JAM!

How to use this site:

• Simply navigate to the video of your choice in the sidebar (If you are viewing on mobile, you will have to open the sidebar with the button on the top right corner).

• Our subtitles will automatically appear on the video. If you see double subtitles, ensure that closed captions (CC) of the original YouTube video is turned OFF.

• To enable fullscreen mode, please use the button below the video (Due to how the subtitles are displayed, the usual fullscreen button on the YouTube player is disabled).

Disclaimer:

• These subtitles are unofficial fan contributions, and may contain inaccuracies.

• We are not a sub team, hence subtitles contributed by different translators may have varying styles/formats.

• This site cannot support YouTube Shorts or YouTube videos where embed is disabled by uploader.
