This site allows JO1's official YouTube videos to be viewed with subtitles, while having the views counted towards the original video! The subtitles here are created by JAM, for JAM! We hope this can also act as a database to easily navigate JO1's YouTube videos available with subtitles.

このサイトでは、JO1の公式YouTube動画を字幕付きで視聴できます。動画は再投稿ではなく埋め込みなので、再生回数は公式動画の方にカウントされます！ ここにある字幕は全部様々なJAM翻訳者が海外JAMさんのために作りました。海外JAMさんも一緒にJO1コンテンツを楽しみましょう！

How to use this site

• On the sidebar (on mobile: open the sidebar with the ☰ button), select a video category and a dropdown list of episodes/videos should appear. Select your video of choice.
• Subtitles will appear automatically. If you see double captions, ensure that closed captions (CC) of the original YouTube video is turned OFF.
• To enable fullscreen mode, please use the button below the video (Due to how the subtitles are displayed, the usual fullscreen button on the YouTube player is disabled).
• If you wish to contribute subs, please check the 'Contribute Subtitles' page for more details.

Disclaimer

• These subtitles are unofficial fan contributions, and may contain inaccuracies.
• We are not a sub team, hence subtitles contributed by different translators may have varying styles/formats.
• This site cannot support YouTube Shorts or YouTube videos where embed is disabled by the uploader.
