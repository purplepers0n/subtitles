This site allows JO1's YouTube videos to be viewed with English subtitles, while having the views counted towards the original video!
These subtitles are created by JAM, for JAM! Do note that these subtitles are not official, and may contain inaccuracies.

To use this site, simply navigate to the video of your choice in the sidebar (If you are viewing on mobile, you will have to open the sidebar with the button on the top right corner).
Our subtitles will automatically appear on the video. If you see double subtitles, ensure that closed captions (CC) of the oringal YouTube video is turned <OFF.
